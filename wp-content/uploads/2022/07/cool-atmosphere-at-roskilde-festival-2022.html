    Cool atmosphere at Roskilde festival 2022

    Jul 21, 2022 11:00:00 PM

    ArcticStore refrigerated containers made an appearance once again at the Roskilde festival in Roskilde, Denmark. 

    The Roskilde Festival is one of the biggest musical festivals in the world and has taken place at the Roskilde Animal Scene between the 25th of June and the 2nd of July 2022. The star-studded lineup had notable performers including AC/DC, Dua Lipa, Post Malone and many more. 

    TITAN Containers is proud to have provided its best Arcticstore freezer containers and supplied refrigerated storage solutions to keep all the food and refreshments nice and cold!

    It has been an honour to be able to play a role in creating the "Cool Atmosphere" at the Roskilde Festival alongside the artists and the 130,000 attendees and fans for the 5th year in a row!
